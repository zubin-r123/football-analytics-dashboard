# Football Analytics Dashboard ⚽

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing player and team 
performance across Europe's top 5 football leagues for the 
2024–25 season.

---

## Overview

![Dashboard Overview](screenshots/overview.png)

---

## Dataset

- **Source:** [Kaggle / FBref] (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/hubertsidorowicz/football-players-stats-2024-2025)
- **Coverage:** 2,700+ players across the Premier League, La Liga, 
  Serie A, Bundesliga, and Ligue 1
- **Scale:** ~23 matches per player on average
- **Metrics:** Goals, assists, appearances, minutes played, 
  player position, league

---

## Dashboard Features

- **League filter** — drill down into any of the top 5 leagues
- **Position filter** — compare across forwards, midfielders, 
  defenders
- **Player-level analysis** — individual performance breakdown
- **Top performers** — ranked views for goals and assists
- **Scoring trends** — patterns across the season
- **Assist patterns** — creative player identification

## Key Insights

- Salah was shown to have the highest output(Goals + Assists) per matches played among all players
- Bruno Fernandes had the highest Creative passing metrics among all midfielders
- The premier league proved to be the highest scoring league however the bundesliga had a higher Goals/90 meaning there were more goals in less matches played
- Mile Svilar is a good pick for a young emerging Goalkeeper having highest cleen sheets(16) and save percentage(79%) for a 24 year old GK

## How to Open

1. Download `Football_Analytics.pbix`
2. Open with [Power BI Desktop](https://powerbi.microsoft.com/desktop/) 
   (free)
3. Use the filters on the left panel to explore by league, 
   position, or player
